Presence Detector, 2016

Processing program, Projector, Microsoft Kinect, Arduino network

A Kinect detects the presence and movement of the viewer. This data is collected and translated into a live black and white visualisation which continuously scrolls around the installation space. Static images of the visualisations this artwork produces can be found here.

Produced during a year long research project at the University of Huddersfield
